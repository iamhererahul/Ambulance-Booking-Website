Medicare Schemes & Ambulance Management

Medicare Schemes

Health Insurance Coverage (HIC) Scheme

This scheme provides health insurance coverage to eligible family members.Partner with health insurance providers to offer discounted or subsidized health insurance plans specifically tailored for low-income families. This can include coverage for ambulance services as part of the package.

Health Education and Awareness Campaigns (HEAC) Scheme

Utilize the ambulance management system to disseminate health education materials and raise awareness about preventive healthcare measures within impoverished communities. Empower families with knowledge to take better care of their health and utilize available healthcare services effectively.

Ambulance Management

Avail ambulance services for 99/- rupees per family having annual income less than 5lakh
