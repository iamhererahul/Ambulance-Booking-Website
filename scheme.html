Hospitalization Financial Assistance Schemes

Government Scheme Description Coverage Amount Reference Link
Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) Aims to provide health insurance coverage of up to ₹5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization to poor families. Up to ₹5 lakhs per family per year PMJAY
Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY) Provides health insurance coverage for below-poverty-line (BPL) families, offering coverage for hospitalization expenses up to ₹30,000 per family per year. Up to ₹30,000 per family per year RSBY
Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) Offers accidental insurance coverage of up to ₹2 lakhs for accidental death and permanent total disability and up to ₹1 lakh for permanent partial disability. Up to ₹2 lakhs for accidental death PMSBY
Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme Implemented in various states, offers health insurance coverage for poor families, covering hospitalization and other medical expenses. Varies by state Varies by state
